# Domestic Insurance — Admin Focus Underwriting Managers

## Product Overview

Admin Focus Underwriting Managers offers a comprehensive Domestic Insurance product, known internally as Personal Focus, designed for South African homeowners and tenants who prioritise quality protection over the lowest possible premium.

Admin Focus is an FSP (FSP 50086) that underwrites a selective portfolio of personal lines insurance. The Domestic product is one of four specialist lines alongside Commercial, Agri, and Hospitality insurance.

The Domestic Insurance product is distributed exclusively through Admin Focus's network of 22 hand-picked partner brokers. It is not available through comparison websites or direct channels. All enquiries must be directed through a registered broker partner.

---

## Coverage — Buildings

The buildings section of the Domestic policy covers the permanent structure of the insured dwelling and any outbuildings on the same erf. Coverage includes:

- Fire, lightning, and explosion
- Storm, wind, water, and hail damage
- Bursting, leaking, or overflowing of water tanks, pipes, or fixed heating apparatus
- Earthquake and earth tremor
- Impact by animals, falling trees, or aircraft
- Theft of fixed equipment (solar panels, geysers, gates) provided forced entry is evidenced
- Accidental damage to underground pipes and electrical cables
- Subsidence and landslip (subject to underwriter approval at inception)

Buildings are insured on a reinstatement value basis. The policyholder is required to ensure the sum insured reflects the current rebuilding cost, not the market value of the property. Admin Focus recommends a professional valuation every three years.

---

## Coverage — Contents

The contents section covers all moveable possessions belonging to the policyholder and permanent residents of the household, while they are within the insured premises. Coverage includes:

- All-risk cover for theft (with evidence of forcible entry)
- Accidental damage to contents including electronics, furniture, and appliances
- Fire, storm, and water damage to contents
- Power surge cover for electronic equipment (limited to R50,000 per event)
- Geyser contents damage resulting from burst

Contents are valued on a replacement cost basis (new for old) up to the sum insured. Jewellery, watches, and unset stones must be individually specified above R25,000 per item. Fine art and collectables must be separately endorsed and valued.

---

## Coverage — All-Risk (Portable Possessions)

The all-risk section extends cover to portable possessions taken outside the insured premises, anywhere in the world for up to 90 consecutive days. Items typically included:

- Laptops and tablets
- Mobile phones and smart devices
- Jewellery and watches (specified items)
- Cameras and photographic equipment
- Sports equipment and bicycles (limit R15,000 per item unless specified)
- Musical instruments
- Prescription spectacles and hearing aids

All-risk cover operates on an accidental damage and theft basis. Unexplained disappearance and mysterious loss are not covered under the standard policy but can be endorsed for specified items.

---

## Liability Cover

The Domestic policy includes personal liability cover of up to R5,000,000 per occurrence. This protects the policyholder and household members against claims by third parties for:

- Bodily injury caused by the policyholder or a household member
- Property damage caused by the policyholder or a household member
- Liability arising from domestic animals (excluding livestock)

Liability does not extend to motor vehicles, watercraft, or aircraft. Professional indemnity and business liability are excluded from the Domestic product — brokers should refer clients to the Commercial product for these exposures.

---

## Exclusions — Key Items

The following are excluded from all sections of the Domestic policy unless specifically endorsed:

- Mechanical or electrical breakdown of appliances (a separate maintenance warranty can be arranged through AFUM's partner network)
- Wear and tear, gradual deterioration, or corrosion
- Losses arising from intentional acts by the policyholder or any household member
- War, terrorism, and civil commotion (standard market exclusion)
- Nuclear risk
- Damage caused by insects, vermin, or tree roots
- Loss or damage to motor vehicles, caravans, trailers, or watercraft
- Consequential loss or loss of use
- Business property or stock stored at the insured premises above R10,000

## Geyser Cover

Geyser replacement is one of the most common domestic claims in South Africa. Admin Focus includes geyser cover under the buildings section as standard:

- Burst geyser: full replacement of the geyser unit, labour, and call-out
- Water damage resulting from a burst geyser: covered under buildings contents
- Failure due to scale, corrosion, or age beyond 10 years: subject to assessment

Admin Focus operates a 24-hour emergency plumbing assistance line through AF Assist (0861 999 007). Policyholders must contact AF Assist before commissioning independent contractors, or the claim may be repudiated on the grounds of non-authorised repairs.

---

## Solar Panel and Alternative Energy Cover

In response to load-shedding and the growth of rooftop solar in South Africa, Admin Focus has extended its Domestic product to include:

- Solar PV panels (fixed to the structure): covered under buildings
- Inverters and batteries (fixed installations): covered under buildings up to R150,000
- Portable inverters and power stations: covered under contents
- Theft of solar equipment: covered under buildings with evidence of forced entry or sabotage
- Damage by lightning to solar and inverter systems: covered under all-risk electrical surge endorsement

Policyholders must declare the full replacement value of solar installations at inception. Underinsurance penalties apply.

## Claims Process

All Domestic claims must be reported through the broker. Policyholders should not contact Admin Focus directly; the claims process is:

1. Policyholder contacts their broker
2. Broker logs the claim via the Shorekeeper portal (or calls AF Assist for emergencies)
3. Admin Focus claims team acknowledges within 24 business hours
4. Assessor or emergency contractor dispatched (within 4 hours for AF Assist emergencies)
5. Claim settled or repudiated with written reasons within 30 business days for non-complex claims

For emergency situations (burst pipes, fire, break-in), policyholders should call AF Assist directly on 0861 999 007, available 24 hours, 7 days a week.

## Premium Factors

Premiums for the Domestic product are rated on the following factors:

- Construction type (brick, thatch, timber, mixed)
- Roof type (tiles, IBR, thatch)
- Location risk zone (based on AFUM proprietary geographic risk model)
- Security features (alarm, armed response, electric fence, security gates)
- Sum insured (buildings, contents, all-risk)
- Claim history (last 3 years)
- Excess selection (voluntary excess available to reduce premium)

Thatch roof properties attract a loading and require specific fire extinguisher placement. Sectional title properties require confirmation of the body corporate policy to avoid duplication of buildings cover.

---

## Excess Structure

The Domestic policy applies the following standard excesses:

| Section | Standard Excess |
|---------|----------------|
| Buildings | R2,500 or 1% of claim (whichever is greater) |
| Contents — theft | R2,500 |
| Contents — other | R1,500 |
| All-risk | R1,500 per item |
| Geyser | R2,500 per event |
| Power surge | R2,500 per event |
| Personal liability | Nil |

Voluntary excess options are available at inception to reduce premiums. Compulsory excesses may apply for high-risk zones or properties with prior claims.

## Eligibility and Underwriting Criteria

Admin Focus applies a selective underwriting approach. The following may result in referral to the underwriter or decline:

- Properties with active thatch roofing in high-risk fire zones
- Properties with prior claims exceeding 3 in 36 months
- Properties with a replacement value above R8,000,000 (available on referral)
- Properties used for business purposes (bed and breakfast above 4 rooms — refer to Hospitality product)
- Properties in areas with a crime index above AFUM threshold (available on referral with enhanced security endorsement)
- Unoccupied properties for more than 30 consecutive days without prior notification

## Regulatory Notes

Admin Focus Underwriting Managers is authorised as a Financial Services Provider under the FAIS Act (FSP 50086). All advice and intermediary services provided through AFUM's partner broker network must be delivered by brokers holding a current FAIS licence with the appropriate category for personal lines.

Brokers are responsible for conducting a needs analysis before placing cover. The Domestic product is not suitable for:
- Commercial properties
- Short-term rental properties (Airbnb, VRBO) with more than 90 rental nights per year — refer to Hospitality product
- Agricultural properties with farming operations — refer to Agri product
